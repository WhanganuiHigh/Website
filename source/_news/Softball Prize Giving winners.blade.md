<p>Congratulations to our two Whanganui High School players that Represented Manawatu at the Under 17 Girls Nationals held in Palmerston North in January 2017.</p>
<p>At the Manawatu Softball Prize Giving on Wednesday 15 March, Leighton Kotuhi-Brown (captain) was named Top Fielder and Shanice Campbell was awarded the Most Valuable Player trophy.</p>
