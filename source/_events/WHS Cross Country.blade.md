<h4>WHS Cross Country&nbsp;</h4>
<p><strong>Venue:</strong> Whanganui High School</p>
<p><strong>Format for Cross Country:</strong></p>
<p><strong>12.50pm</strong> - End of P3<br />Get changed and meet at the back of gyms.<br />House areas allocated!&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>1.10pm</strong> - Junior Girls Race<br /><strong>1.30pm</strong> - Y9 Girls<br /><strong>1.50pm</strong> - Y9 Boys<br /><strong>2.10pm</strong> - Junior Boys<br /><strong>2.30pm</strong> - Senior Girls<br /><strong>2.50pm</strong> - Senior Boys&nbsp;</p>
<p>Once your race is finished you will remain in the house areas until ALL races are run.<br />Bring snacks and water bottle with you! <br />Wear house colours and support your housemates as they compete.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span>If you are in PED3, SPS3, SPX3, PED2, SPX2, PED1 or PEXO this event is COMPULSORY and your PE teacher has signed you up!&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span>Age groups and distance of run are as follows:</span><br /><span>Y9 Girls - 2.5km</span><br /><span>Y9 Boys - 2.5km</span><br /><span>Junior Girls (U16 as at 31 Dec 2019) - 2.5km</span><br /><span>Junior Boys (U16 as at 31 Dec 2019) - 3km</span><br /><span>Senior Girls - 3km</span><br /><span>Senior Boys - 3.9km</span><br /><br /><span>House Points: 1 house point for each entry. Top 10 placings 1st = 10pts, 2nd = 9pts, 3rd = 8pts.....</span></p>
